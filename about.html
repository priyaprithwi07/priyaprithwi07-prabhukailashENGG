About Prabhu Kailash Engineering College

Prabhu Kailash Engineering College (PKEC) was established in 2010 under the Prabhu Kailash Educational & Charitable Trust with a vision to provide quality technical education in the Magadh region of Bihar.

PKEC is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and affiliated to Bihar Engineering University (BEU), Patna. The college offers 6 B.Tech. programmes with an intake of 60 students each.

Located at Kailash Nagar, Santhua More, Aurangabad, the college boasts a sprawling 10‑acre self‑owned campus with modern laboratories, a well‑stocked central library, separate hostels for boys and girls, a 600‑seat air‑conditioned auditorium and extensive sports facilities.
