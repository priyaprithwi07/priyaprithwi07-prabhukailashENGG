Campus Infrastructure

World‑Class Facilities at PKEC

Designed for holistic student development – from academics to sports, hostel to healthcare

Laboratories

20 well‑equipped department‑wise labs with the latest instruments. Computer labs with 160+ Core i5/i7 systems, 8GB RAM and 1 Gbps internet connectivity.

Central Library

16,000+ books, 500+ journals, e‑resources and a 150‑seater reading hall. Stocked with technical references, project reports and digital resources.

Hostels

Boys hostel (200 seats) and girls hostel (100 seats) with hygienic mess, common room, 24/7 security, CCTV and dedicated female warden for girls.

Auditorium

600‑seat fully air‑conditioned auditorium with HD projector, professional PA system and modern AV setup for events, seminars and convocations.

Classrooms

32 spacious classrooms with HD projectors, whiteboards and seating capacity of 60 students each. 8 tutorial/seminar rooms with 30‑seat capacity.

Wi‑Fi Campus

Full campus Wi‑Fi with 100 Mbps leased line and 40 Mbps backup. High‑speed internet in classrooms, library, hostels and all common areas.

Transport

8 college buses covering Aurangabad city, Daudnagar, Obra, Rafiganj and Nabinagar for safe and convenient daily commute.

Medical Room

Resident nurse with basic medicines and first‑aid facility. Tie‑up with Sadar Hospital Aurangabad for emergency medical care.

Sports Facilities

Cricket ground, volleyball court, badminton, table tennis and indoor games. Annual sports events and inter‑college tournaments organised.

Cafeteria

FSSAI‑compliant cafeteria serving 800+ students daily with hygienic and nutritious meals. Separate seating for boys, girls and faculty.

CCTV Surveillance

96 cameras across the entire campus providing 24/7 monitoring and ensuring safety of all students, staff and visitors.

Power Supply

33 KV substation with 500 KVA DG backup ensuring uninterrupted power supply across the entire campus including hostels and labs.
