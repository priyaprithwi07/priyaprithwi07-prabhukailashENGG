About PKEC

Empowering Students Through Quality Technical Education

Prabhu Kailash Engineering College (PKEC) is a premier AICTE‑approved and BEU‑affiliated engineering institution located in Aurangabad, Bihar. Established in 2010 under the Prabhu Kailash Educational & Charitable Trust, PKEC is committed to delivering world‑class technical education.

Under the visionary leadership of Chairman Mr. Santosh Shrishtav and Principal Mr. Vikash Kumar, PKEC has grown into a reputed institution with 77 experienced faculty across 6 engineering departments on a sprawling 10‑acre campus.
